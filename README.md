# DoubleSketch

[![DoubleSketch Logo](https://doublesketchgame.onrender.com/img/logo.png)](https://doublesketchgame.onrender.com)

A small multiplayer social game where two players draw and one must be identified as the real artist.

Two drawers receive different prompts — one real, one fake.  
Guessers must figure out which drawing matches the real prompt!

🎮 **Play here:** [DoubleSketch](https://doublesketchgame.onrender.com)

---

## How to Play

1. Create a room.
2. Share the room code with your friends.
3. Two players are assigned roles: **Real Drawer** and **Decoy Drawer** — the rest are **Guessers**.
4. The Real Drawer draws their prompt honestly. The Decoy Drawer's goal is to sabotage the guessers by turning the real drawing into their own fake prompt.
5. Guessers study the drawing and submit guesses for what the real prompt is.
6. Reveal the answer and see who guessed correctly!

Players join using room codes and play rounds of **DoubleSketch** together in real time.
